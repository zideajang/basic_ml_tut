### 两点分布(0-1 分布)
| X  |  1 | 0  |
|---|---|---|
| p  | p  | 1 - p  |

$$ E(x) = 1 \cdot p + 0 \cdot q = p$$
$$ D(X) = E(X^2) - [E(X)]^2 = 1^2 \cdot p + 0^2 \cdot (1-p) - p^2 = pq$$

### 二项分布
- 期望 $E(x) = np$
- 方差 $D(x) = npq = np(1-q)$

$$ X \{ x_1,x_2, \dots ,x_i \} $$
